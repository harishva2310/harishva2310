A Reddit and YouTube Crawler for analyzing US Political and Crime Data

As a part of my Masters degree at SUNY Binghamton, I developed a Reddit and YouTube crawler to analyze political and crime related data posted on these 2 platforms with a team of 4. Using Python and MongoDB, we designed an always-on application and collected nearly 3 months of text data such as comments, posts from these platforms amounting to nearly 3 million comments. Using Machine-Learning and AI algorithms such as Naive-Bayes Classifier, Regressions, NLP, ModerateHateSpeech API we derived some interesting insights from these platforms. We saw that there was a political party subreddit with twice the number of comments classified as toxic than other parties' subreddits and political content on both platforms was significantly higher than crime content.
